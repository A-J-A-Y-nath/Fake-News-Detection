Welcome to the Fake News Detection Project!

Our mission is to help people identify fake news and prevent the spread of misinformation. This web-based platform analyzes news articles using machine learning models like Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Gradient Boosting, and Random Forest to determine their credibility.

Features

  • Fake News Detection: Analyze and verify news articles for authenticity.
  • Machine Learning Models: Uses Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Gradient Boosting, and Random Forest for classification.
  • Credibility Score: Assigns a confidence score based on model predictions.
  • Web-Based Platform: Provides an easy-to-use interface for checking news authenticity.
  • Fast and Efficient: Quickly processes news articles and delivers results.
  • Open for Improvement: Continuously evolving with user feedback and new features.

Technologies Used

  • Machine Learning: Implements various models to detect fake news.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Processes and analyzes textual data.
  • Python: Handles backend logic and model execution.
  • Flask: Powers the web-based interface.
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript: Builds the frontend for user interaction.

How It Works

Our fake news detection system works in three simple steps:

  1. Input: Users submit a news article or link for analysis.
  2. Processing: The system analyzes the text, checks the source, and evaluates the credibility of the content.
  3. Output: Users receive a detailed report with a credibility score and recommendations.
